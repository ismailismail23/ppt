How to modify your PowerPoint Presentation templates

  1. How to add background on your cover slide

    • click icon placeholder picture in the middle

    • Select the image you want to make as the background on the slide cover, and then click insert or click 2x that picture

    • it will look like this

    • therefore right-click on the image click send to back

    • then the appearance will be like this

    if you want to change color of the text

    • Click the text box of the text

    • Click in the direction pointed by the mouse pointer

    • choose the color you want

    NB : The send to back menu only applies to placeholders on the cover slide and ending slide because there are dark text box, logos, and maybe transparent rectangle dark color. For the next slide when using placeholders there is no need to use the send to back menu

  2. How to add or placeholder image in slide

    • click icon placeholder picture

    • Select the image you want to make as the placeholder on the slide, and then click insert or click 2x that picture

    • the image will be precise and not broken

  3. How to change background color in slide

    • right-click on the backgorund then click format background

    • click fill color

    • choose the color according to your wishes

  4. How to choose the color on the menu or anothere shapes options in the slide

    • click the menu made of shapes

    • right-click on the menu then click send to back. (because the first layer is a textbox)

    • click again the shape or menu and then right-click on the menu then click format shape

    • choose the color according to your wishes

    • and then right click the shapes and click send to back. so that the textbox in menu appears

  5. How to change the color of the icon in the slide

    • right click the icon and click format graphic

    • choose the color according to your wishes
